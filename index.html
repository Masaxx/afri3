How It Works

Simple for everyone

Whether you're moving cargo or hauling it, getting started takes minutes.

1

Create a free account

Register as a Shipping Entity. No payment required — it's completely free for those posting loads.

2

Post your freight job

Fill in cargo details — type, weight, pickup location, delivery destination, date, and your price.

3

Review applicants

Subscribed carriers apply to your job. Review their profiles and accept the best match for your shipment.

1

Register your company

Sign up as a Trucking Company. Provide your fleet size, cargo capabilities, and business registration.

2

Subscribe via PayPal

Pay a monthly subscription via PayPal to unlock access to all available freight jobs on the platform.

3

Apply for loads

Browse available jobs, filter by cargo type, route or industry, and apply to the ones that fit your fleet.
